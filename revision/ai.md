## AI Tools and Techniques

- **LLM:** Large Languae Models trained on massive data. (eg, chatgpt)
- **Gen AI:** New content generated from scratch. (eg, nano banana, veo 3)
- **Multimodel AI:** Handles text, images, audio together (eg, chatgpt)
- **Prompt Engg:** Talking clearly to AI.
- **AGI:** Airtificial General Intelligence. (AI becomes conscious)
- **Hallucination:** Creating random and fake info.
- **AI Agents:** AI software for specific tasks.

**How AI works**

- Precdicts next word based on the provided context.
- input converts into tokens, tokens into numbers and these are processed by AI model.
- After processing it generates the output.

**Types of AI**
- Level 1: Standalone AI - used directly like chatgpt.
- Level 2: Integrated AI - ai capabilities build into existing s/w like email assistant.
- Level 3: Custom AI - Tailored AI solution developed for specific business needs.

**Vague Prompt**
```
help me prepare for an interview  for a technical role
```
**Prompt Frameworks**
- **AIM Framework**
- A: Actor(who is the AI?)
- I: Input(What Information)
- M: Mission(Whats the goal?)

```
I'm a assistant incubation manager at IIMT Business Incubator Foundation, Now I want to apply in the Y-combinator for the relavent position, Please help me prepare for the same.
```

- **RCAF Framework**
- R: Role(Define AI's expertise)
- C: Context(Provide Background)
- A: Ask(State your request)
- F: Format(Specify O/p style)

```
Act as a experienced recrutor at Y-Combinator.

I'm a assistant incubation manager at IIMT Business Incubator Foundation with an experience of 1 year. 

I want to apply in the Y-combinator for the relavent job position, 

Please help me prepare for interview. I want to you ask question and evaluate my answers.
```
